D.I.G

A power trio from Sweden formed in 2013 with Elias Båld on drums, Fredrik Bergqvist on bass and Björn Holmström on vocals/guitar. D.I.G recorded their debut album by them self in 2015 and an EP in 2019. During 2019 D.I.G parted ways with Elias who went on become the new drummer for punk band "Röda hund". Elias was replaced by exellent drummer Jonathan Lindström, a drummer also from Bengtsfors now returning home from the darkest north of Sweden. The D.I.G boys are still creating heavy groove rock and planing to release new music during 2020.
